# 03 — Debate Log (Full Transcript)
## Most Feasible Architecture for AI Agents in Threat Modeling and Threat Hunting

**Date:** 2026-06-11
**Motion:** For a security team building AI agents across threat modeling and threat hunting today, the most feasible architecture is a portfolio of narrow, per-task deterministic pipelines — each framework-as-state-machine, retrieval-grounded, paired with an independent critic, and human-gated, deployed incrementally — rather than a single unified supervisor/hierarchical multi-agent platform spanning all tasks.

- **Debater 1 (Position A):** Narrow deterministic pipelines, human-gated, incrementally deployed. *Affirms.* Speaks first each round.
- **Debater 2 (Position B):** Unified supervisor/hierarchical multi-agent platform across all tasks. *Opposes.* Speaks second.
- **Judge:** Orchestrator, applying the weighted rubric in `02_criteria.md` (Feasibility 25 · Reliability/safety 25 · Evidential grounding 20 · Cost/latency 12 · Extensibility 10 · Auditability 8).
