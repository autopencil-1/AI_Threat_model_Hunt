## Outcome

**Debate ran 6 rounds (of 20 max), early-stopped on durable mutual convergence.** The provisional R4 convergence was *reopened by the judge* because it concealed a load-bearing contradiction; Rounds 5–6 resolved that plus two further seams (Tier-2 reasoner safety; shared-Store read/write policy). The motion was *refined*, not won outright by either pure position.

- **Rounds tally:** Debater 2 edged the contested exchanges **1 – 2** (D1 won R1 narrow; D2 won R2 clear + R3 narrow by repeatedly identifying what pure Position A structurally could not do; R4/R5/R6 convergence-and-refinement draws). On *architecture*, D1's framework is the structural backbone and held the two heaviest criteria, Feasibility (25) + Cost (12), throughout; on *conceptual battles*, D2's reasoning-coordination insight repeatedly forced the refinements.
- **Architecture outcome:** **Debater 1's deterministic framework is the structural backbone** (per-task framework-as-state-machine graphs, proven-blueprint incremental rollout, grounding-constrained cost/audit discipline, autonomous-disposer forbidden). **Debater 2's load-bearing insight is grafted in** (the open-world cross-task pivot space requires a bounded, grounded reasoning core — present in *both* dispatcher tiers; one shared immutable-KG substrate beats duplicated silos; MAST "structured coordination").
- **Converged architecture (final, after R5–R6 refinements):** N deterministic per-task graphs + per-task external critics, over **one immutable read-only KG grounding substrate** (CVE→CWE→CAPEC→ATT&CK, agents never write it) + **one injection boundary** + **one audit schema** + **one append-only, sign-off-gated, provenance-tracked artifact store** (cross-task reuse as confidence-weighted hypotheses, never as grounding). Cross-task coupling via **one HITL-gated tiered dispatcher**: tier-1 = deterministic KG-frontier retrieval + a thin grounded re-ranker (edge-bound); tier-2 = bounded open-generation reasoner on genuine no-edge miss; both halt at `interrupt()` (human = sole disposer), gate surfaces *evidence not verdicts*, with mandatory KG-citation (→ off-graph injection detector), independent-critic re-check, and default-deny below a confidence floor; validated novel pivots promoted into KG edges; default tier set empirically by an **instrumented miss-rate**. Rollout: D1 + D3 → ATT&CK/KG infra → D2/D4 → D5 copilot. No always-on autonomous supervisor; no ungated agent-to-agent collaboration; no autonomous hunting.
